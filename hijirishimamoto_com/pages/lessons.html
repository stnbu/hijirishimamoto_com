Lessons

I offer lessons from 5 years old and up at my location in Milpitas, CA or online. I teach beginner to advanced level for Saxophone and beginner to intermediate level for Piano and Clarinet. I have been teaching music since 1996. I’m classically trained and especially love and have a lot of experience for wind ensemble. I try to have fun and interesting lessons. If you are interested, please contact me though Contact page.
