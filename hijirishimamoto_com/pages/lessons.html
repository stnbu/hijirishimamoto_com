Lessons

I offer lessons for students ages six and up at my location in Milpitas, California or online. I teach beginner to advanced level saxophone and beginner to intermediate level piano and clarinet. I have been teaching music since 1996. I am classically trained and have a special love for and much experience with wind ensembles. I try to have fun and interesting lessons. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me.
